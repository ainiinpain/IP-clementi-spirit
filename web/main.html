About

Clementi is located between Jurong West and Dover. The name Clementi was named after Sir Cecil Clementi Smith, the Governer if the Straits Settlement from 1887 to 1893. Some parts of Clementi used to be swamp land. The other part was made up of kampungs(Docks) and villages with squatter-tyoe homes, backyard trades, cottage industries and farms.
