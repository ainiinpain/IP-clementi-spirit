About Clementi Community Centre

Located within 10 minutes walking distance from Clementi MRT Station and Bus Interchange. Clementi CC is host to a SingPost POP Station to better serve the needs of our residents. It also serves as a Centre where everyone at any age group can do their activities or just simply to hang out!
